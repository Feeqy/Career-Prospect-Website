My Experience in SP

Learning

Lessons and Timetable

My teachers and classmates play a big role in my learning. Being into this course for almost four weeks, I can say my teachers have been friendly enough to ask help from and ask any pending questions I might have. My class timetable is also kind of packed except for cetain days. Some of the lessons are actually quite long that I would not be able to sit through the class without fidgeting. Overall I can certainly say I am so far having a wonderful experience in SP.

Food

Eateries

Entering SP, I was not sure what to expect for the food sold there. Since I am a muslim, I was doubtful whether there would be enough muslim stalls to eat from. Fortunately there were sufficient but not in the food court in my block. Moreover there were also fast food restaurants to eat from such as Macdonalds and Subway, so I think food is not a big issue for me anymore.
